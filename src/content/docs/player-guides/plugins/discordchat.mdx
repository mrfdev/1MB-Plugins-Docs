---
title: DiscordChat Commands
description: Player-friendly command guide for DiscordChat.
---

`/discordchat` DiscordSRV server-chat engagement tracker with quality-gated EXP, conversation clusters, first-Discord broadcasts, community pulse, recurring bonus windows, linked-account streaks, top lists, point rewards, safe item tools, dependency-aware reward checks, opt-out reminders, milestone celebration privacy, and staff smoke/community/economy/archive/award reports.

## Commands

  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Command</th>
      <th>What it does</th>
      <th>Example</th>

  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/discordchat</code></td>
      <td>Opens the DiscordChat overview GUI with points, streak, tracked words/emotes, reminders, rewards, and milestones.</td>
      <td><code>/discordchat</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/discordchat status</code></td>
      <td>Shows your linked state, points, pending EXP, current streak, longest streak, and tracked chat totals.</td>
      <td><code>/discordchat status</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/discordchat top [points|streak|messages|words|emotes]</code></td>
      <td>Shows the top 10 DiscordChat profiles for a public metric.</td>
      <td><code>/discordchat top</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/discordchat pulse</code></td>
      <td>Opens the community pulse GUI with today's activity, seven-day totals, and active XP boosts. Console receives the same data as chat output.</td>
      <td><code>/discordchat pulse</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/discordchat rewards</code></td>
      <td>Opens the point trade GUI.</td>
      <td><code>/discordchat rewards</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/discordchat tools</code></td>
      <td>Opens the point-funded item tools GUI for Unbreaking IV, Fortune IV, and Looting IV.</td>
      <td><code>/discordchat tools</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/discordchat milestones</code></td>
      <td>Shows the configured streak milestones and your next target.</td>
      <td><code>/discordchat milestones</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/discordchat history</code></td>
      <td>Opens the milestone history GUI with hit dates and bonus point details where available.</td>
      <td><code>/discordchat history</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/discordchat link</code></td>
      <td>Explains /discord and /discord link so players can join and link DiscordSRV.</td>
      <td><code>/discordchat link</code></td>

    <tr>
      <td><code>/discordchat optout</code></td>
