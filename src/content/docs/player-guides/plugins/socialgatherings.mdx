This page introduces SocialGatherings from a public server-docs point of view. It focuses on what the feature does in-game, how to get started when you have access, and which commands are useful.

## What It Does

SocialGatherings rewards players for meeting up and doing small themed activities together. Some gatherings may happen around town builds such as campfires, dinner tables, beaches, markets, farms, or fishing spots.

## How Players Use It

Start with /gathering. The sections below explain what SocialGatherings is for, which commands players can try, and what to expect when your account has access.

Available features include:

- Small themed group activities around campfires, dinners, fishing spots, farms, markets, beaches, and town builds.
- A gathering-type browser showing the social activities that are currently enabled.
- Nearby-area detection that explains whether the current location can host a gathering.
- Personal opt-in and opt-out controls for gathering participation.

## Quick Start

1. Start with `/gathering` to open or view SocialGatherings.
2. Use `/gathering help` when you want the in-game help or a short explanation.
3. Try `/gathering info` when you are ready to use one of the feature actions.
