This page introduces SocialGatherings from a player point of view. It focuses on what you can do in-game, how to get started, and which commands are useful when the feature is available to you.

## What It Does

SocialGatherings rewards players for meeting up and doing small themed activities together. Some gatherings may happen around town builds such as campfires, dinner tables, beaches, markets, farms, or fishing spots.

## How Players Use It

Start in-game with /gathering. The sections below explain what SocialGatherings is for, which commands players can try, and what to expect when your account has access.

## Things You Can Do

- Use /gathering to see the main gathering information.
- Use /gathering types to browse gathering themes when they are enabled.
- Use /gathering nearby to see whether your current location matches an active gathering area.
- Use /gathering toggle if you want to opt in or out of gathering participation.

## Quick Start

1. Start with `/gathering` to open or view SocialGatherings.
2. Use `/gathering help` when you want the in-game help or a short explanation.
3. Try `/gathering info` when you are ready to use one of the feature actions.
