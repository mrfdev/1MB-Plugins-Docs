## How Players Use It

Start in-game with /refer. The sections below explain what Refer is for, which commands players can try, and what to expect when your account has access.

## Things You Can Do

- Open or view Refer with /refer.
- Check your own Refer progress or settings with /refer status.

## Quick Start

1. Start with `/refer` to open or view Refer.
2. Use `/refer status` to check your current progress, settings, or state.
3. Use `/refer info` when you want the in-game help or a short explanation.
4. Try `/refer Steve` when you are ready to use one of the feature actions.
