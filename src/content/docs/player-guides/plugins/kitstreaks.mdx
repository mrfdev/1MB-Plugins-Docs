---
title: KitStreaks Commands
description: Player-friendly command guide for KitStreaks.
---

KitStreaks listens for CMI kit claim events and turns them into configurable streaks, milestone calendars, and optional rewards.

## Commands

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Command</th>
      <th>What it does</th>
      <th>Example</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/kitstreak info</code></td>
      <td>Shows a friendly explanation of what KitStreaks does.</td>
      <td><code>/kitstreak info</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/kitstreak status [track|kit:&lt;kit&gt;]</code></td>
      <td>Shows your current KitStreaks status.</td>
      <td><code>/kitstreak status</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/kitstreak tracks [page]</code></td>
      <td>Runs the KitStreaks tracks action.</td>
      <td><code>/kitstreak tracks</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/kitstreak kits [page]</code></td>
      <td>Runs the KitStreaks kits action.</td>
      <td><code>/kitstreak kits</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/kitstreak calendar [track|kit:&lt;kit&gt;] [page]</code></td>
      <td>Runs the KitStreaks calendar action.</td>
      <td><code>/kitstreak calendar</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/kitstreak rewards [page]</code></td>
      <td>Shows your KitStreaks rewards view.</td>
      <td><code>/kitstreak rewards</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/kitstreak claim [track|all] [milestone|all]</code></td>
      <td>Claims an available KitStreaks reward.</td>
      <td><code>/kitstreak claim</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/kitstreak top [track] [page]</code></td>
      <td>Shows the KitStreaks leaderboard or top list.</td>
      <td><code>/kitstreak top</code></td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

## Aliases

- `/kitstreaks`
- `/kstreak`

## Examples

- `/kitstreak info`
- `/kitstreak status`
- `/kitstreak status daily`
- `/kitstreak status kit:starter`
- `/kitstreak tracks`
- `/kitstreak kits`
- `/kitstreak calendar`
- `/kitstreak calendar daily`
- `/kitstreak calendar kit:starter`
- `/kitstreak rewards`
- `/kitstreak claim all`
- `/kitstreak claim daily 7`
- `/kitstreak top daily`
