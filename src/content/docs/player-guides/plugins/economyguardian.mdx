This page introduces EconomyGuardian from a public server-docs point of view. It focuses on what the feature does in-game, how to get started when you have access, and which commands are useful.

## What It Does

EconomyGuardian is a read-only server-management plugin that watches server economy balance changes and reports unusual money movement during the current runtime.

## How Players Use It

Start in-game with /economyguardian. The sections below explain what EconomyGuardian is for, which commands players can try, and what to expect when your account has access.

## Things You Can Do

- Listens to CMIUserBalanceChangeEvent at monitor priority.
- Tracks total observed positive and negative balance movement.
- Tracks per-player balance movement and anomaly counts.
- Flags large positive spikes, large negative movement, sharp percent changes, and repeated high-value action/source paths.
- Keeps bounded recent anomaly records.

## Quick Start

1. Start with `/economyguardian` to open or view EconomyGuardian.
2. Use `/economyguardian status` to check your current progress, settings, or state.
3. Try `/economyguardian player Steve` when you are ready to use one of the feature actions.
