Trusted-player `/tpauto` toggle for auto-accepting incoming CMI `/tpa` and `/tpahere` requests.

## Commands

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Command</th>
      <th>What it does</th>
      <th>Example</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/tpauto</code></td>
      <td>Opens or shows the main TPAuto player view.</td>
      <td><code>/tpauto</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/tpauto on</code></td>
      <td>Changes or clears your TPAuto setting.</td>
      <td><code>/tpauto on</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/tpauto off</code></td>
      <td>Changes or clears your TPAuto setting.</td>
      <td><code>/tpauto off</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/tpauto toggle</code></td>
      <td>Changes or clears your TPAuto setting.</td>
      <td><code>/tpauto toggle</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/tpauto status</code></td>
      <td>Shows your current TPAuto status.</td>
      <td><code>/tpauto status</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/tpauto reset</code></td>
      <td>Manages one of your TPAuto entries.</td>
      <td><code>/tpauto reset</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/tpauto info</code></td>
      <td>Shows a friendly explanation of what TPAuto does.</td>
      <td><code>/tpauto info</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/tpauto help</code></td>
      <td>Shows the TPAuto commands available to you.</td>
      <td><code>/tpauto help</code></td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

## Examples

- `/tpauto on`
- `/tpauto status`
- `/tpauto off`
