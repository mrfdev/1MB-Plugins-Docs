`/rate` booster status and admin control for mcMMO, Jobs, and PyroWelcomesPro Points detection, replacing the old standalone Boosters plugin.

## Commands

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Command</th>
      <th>What it does</th>
      <th>Example</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/rate</code></td>
      <td>Opens or shows the main Boosters player view.</td>
      <td><code>/rate</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/rate status</code></td>
      <td>Shows your current Boosters status.</td>
      <td><code>/rate status</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/rate info</code></td>
      <td>Shows a friendly explanation of what Boosters does.</td>
      <td><code>/rate info</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/rate help</code></td>
      <td>Shows the Boosters commands available to you.</td>
      <td><code>/rate help</code></td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

## Examples

- `/rate`
- `/rate info`
