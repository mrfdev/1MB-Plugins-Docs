---
title: JourneyMap Commands
description: Player-friendly command guide for JourneyMap.
---

JourneyMap turns playtime into a light player-facing progress map with eras, badges, milestones, and optional rewards.

## Commands

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Command</th>
      <th>What it does</th>
      <th>Example</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/journeymap</code></td>
      <td>Opens or shows the main JourneyMap player view.</td>
      <td><code>/journeymap</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/journeymap help</code></td>
      <td>Shows the JourneyMap commands available to you.</td>
      <td><code>/journeymap help</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/journeymap status</code></td>
      <td>Shows your current JourneyMap status.</td>
      <td><code>/journeymap status</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/journeymap milestones [page]</code></td>
      <td>Shows your JourneyMap milestones view.</td>
      <td><code>/journeymap milestones</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/journeymap rewards [page]</code></td>
      <td>Shows your JourneyMap rewards view.</td>
      <td><code>/journeymap rewards</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/journeymap claim [id|all]</code></td>
      <td>Claims an available JourneyMap reward.</td>
      <td><code>/journeymap claim</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/journeymap refresh</code></td>
      <td>Runs the JourneyMap refresh action.</td>
      <td><code>/journeymap refresh</code></td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

## Aliases

- `/jmap`
- `/playjourney`

## Examples

- `/journeymap`
- `/journeymap status`
- `/journeymap milestones`
- `/journeymap milestones 2`
- `/journeymap rewards`
- `/journeymap claim all`
- `/journeymap claim settler`
- `/journeymap refresh`
