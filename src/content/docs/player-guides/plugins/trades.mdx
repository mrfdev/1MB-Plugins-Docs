---
title: Trades Commands
description: Player-friendly command guide for Trades.
---

Trades gives players a GUI where configured exchanges can trade required items, money, or EXP levels for command-based rewards. A trade can be a seasonal event exchange, a vote-token hand-in, a one-time kit unlock, or a repeatable resource turn-in.

## Commands

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Command</th>
      <th>What it does</th>
      <th>Example</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/trade</code></td>
      <td>Opens or shows the main Trades player view.</td>
      <td><code>/trade</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/trade info</code></td>
      <td>Shows a friendly explanation of what Trades does.</td>
      <td><code>/trade info</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/trade help</code></td>
      <td>Shows the Trades commands available to you.</td>
      <td><code>/trade help</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/trade open [trade] [player]</code></td>
      <td>Opens a specific Trades menu or entry.</td>
      <td><code>/trade open</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>/trade open category &lt;category&gt; [player]</code></td>
      <td>Opens a specific Trades menu or entry.</td>
      <td><code>/trade open category vote</code></td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

## Examples

- `/trade`
- `/trade info`
- `/trade open summer_event`
- `/trade open category vote`
