---

mcMMO expands everyday Minecraft actions into trainable skills. Mining, excavation, woodcutting, fishing, combat, repair, alchemy, taming, and other activities can gain levels and unlock passive or activated abilities.

> Commands and features on 1MoreBlock depend on the current server configuration, world, and your permissions. A command listed here may be limited to certain ranks or contexts.

## What it adds

- Persistent levels across thirteen configurable skills.
- Passive bonuses and active abilities tied to tools or combat styles.
- Personal statistics, rankings, power level, and inspect tools.
- Parties, party chat, shared experience, and other social features where enabled.

## Commands

| Command | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| `/mcstats` | Shows your skill levels and power level. |
| `/mcrank` | Shows your position in skill rankings. |
| `/mctop` | Shows server-wide mcMMO rankings. |
| `/mcability` | Toggles ability activation messages or use. |
| `/<skill>` | Shows details for a skill, such as `/mining` or `/fishing`. |
| `/party` | Shows party commands where parties are enabled. |
| `/inspect <player>` | Shows another player's visible mcMMO stats when permitted. |

## Getting started

1. Run `/mcstats` to see your current levels.
2. Run a skill command such as `/mining` to read its abilities.
3. Use the relevant tool and normal gameplay actions to train that skill.

## Player notes

- Experience rates, ability formulas, cooldowns, and disabled worlds are configured by 1MoreBlock.
- Abilities do not override region, island, plot, anti-cheat, or economy rules.
- The separate mcMMO More custom project extends API/documentation behavior and is linked elsewhere in this site.
