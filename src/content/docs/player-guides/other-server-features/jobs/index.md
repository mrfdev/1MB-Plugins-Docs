Jobs rewards normal gameplay through professions such as mining, building, farming, hunting, or other roles configured by 1MoreBlock. Each job defines which actions pay, how progression works, and what limits apply.

> Commands and features on 1MoreBlock depend on the current server configuration, world, and your permissions. A command listed here may be limited to certain ranks or contexts.

## What it adds

- Multiple configurable professions and job levels.
- Money, job experience, points, bonuses, and daily limits.
- Job quests, shop, top lists, and activity information.
- Integration with CMI economy, CMILib, Vault, PlaceholderAPI, WorldGuard, and other plugins.

## Commands

| Command | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| `/jobs browse` | Browses available jobs. |
| `/jobs join <job>` | Joins a job when you have an open slot. |
| `/jobs leave <job>` | Leaves a job; progress-retention rules may apply. |
| `/jobs stats` | Shows your job levels and progress. |
| `/jobs info <job>` | Shows configured actions and rewards. |
| `/jobs quests` | Shows available job quests. |
| `/jobs top` | Shows job rankings. |
| `/jobs shop` | Opens the Jobs shop when enabled. |

## Getting started

1. Run `/jobs browse` and compare available professions.
2. Use `/jobs info <job>` before joining.
3. Join a job and play normally; watch action bars or messages for rewards and limits.

## Player notes

- Not every block break, placement, craft, kill, or farm action pays.
- Anti-exploit rules, world restrictions, daily limits, and reduced payouts can apply.
- Leaving a job may reduce or archive progress depending on configuration.
