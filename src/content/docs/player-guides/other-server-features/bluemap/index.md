BlueMap renders selected 1MoreBlock worlds as an interactive 3D map in a web browser. It can show terrain, builds, markers, and selected live information without requiring a client mod.

> Commands and features on 1MoreBlock depend on the current server configuration, world, and your permissions. A command listed here may be limited to certain ranks or contexts.

## What it adds

- Browser-based 3D world map.
- Multiple maps, perspectives, markers, and points of interest.
- Incremental rendering as worlds change.
- Optional live player markers with 1MoreBlock privacy controls.

## Commands

This feature does not require a general player command. It appears automatically, through another menu, or through staff-created content.

## Getting started

1. Open the map link published by 1MoreBlock.
2. Choose a world or map from the map menu.
3. Use search, markers, rotation, tilt, and zoom to explore.

## Player notes

- The map can lag behind live world changes while tiles render.
- Some worlds, regions, or players may be hidden for privacy or gameplay reasons.
- BlueMap is a viewer, not a teleport or claim system.
