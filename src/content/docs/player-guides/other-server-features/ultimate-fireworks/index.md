---
title: "UltimateFireworks Guide"
description: "Add special craftable or reward-based fireworks, launchers, bows, effects, and celebration tools."
---

UltimateFireworks expands fireworks beyond vanilla rockets with configurable special effects and items. 1MoreBlock can use those effects for celebrations, rewards, events, or player-accessible cosmetic fun.

> Commands and features on 1MoreBlock depend on the current server configuration, world, and your permissions. A command listed here may be limited to certain ranks or contexts.

## What it adds

- Custom firework effects and effect combinations.
- Special firework items, launchers, bows, or crafting recipes depending on configuration.
- Permission and world controls for individual effects.
- Console-safe event and celebration tools for staff.

## Commands

This feature does not require a general player command. It appears automatically, through another menu, or through staff-created content.

## Getting started

1. Obtain a configured UltimateFireworks item through an approved source.
2. Read its name and lore before using it.
3. Use it outdoors or in the designated event area and respect other players/builds.

## Player notes

- The exact effects, recipes, items, and commands are version/configuration dependent.
- Some effects can create sound, particles, movement, or area activity and may be restricted by world.
- Do not mass-trigger effects or use them to obscure, disrupt, or lag an area.
