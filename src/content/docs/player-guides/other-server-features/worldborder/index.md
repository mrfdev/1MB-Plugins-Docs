title: "WorldBorder Guide"
description: "Define and enforce world-scale boundaries and support safe border filling or trimming operations."
---

WorldBorder keeps players inside configured world boundaries. It prevents endless terrain expansion, supports predictable map size, and gives staff tools for safe border filling and trimming.

> Commands and features on 1MoreBlock depend on the current server configuration, world, and your permissions. A command listed here may be limited to certain ranks or contexts.

## What it adds

- Per-world border center, radius, shape, and knockback behavior.
- Player movement enforcement and border messages.
- Chunk fill and trim workflows for world preparation.
- Optional portal and shape handling across world environments.

## Commands

This feature does not require a general player command. It appears automatically, through another menu, or through staff-created content.

## Getting started

1. Explore normally within the published world limits.
2. Turn back when the border warns or moves you.
3. Use public travel routes rather than trying to bypass the boundary.

## Player notes

- WorldBorder controls a whole world; BentoBox Border controls individual island boundaries.
- The visible vanilla border and plugin-enforced border can be configured differently.
- World size may change during planned expansions or maintenance.
