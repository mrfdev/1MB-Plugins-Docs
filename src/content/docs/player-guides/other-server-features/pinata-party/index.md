title: "PinataParty Guide"
description: "Run community pinata events where players work together to break a special mob and earn configured rewards."
---

PinataParty creates a celebratory server event around a special pinata entity. Events can be triggered by voting or other milestones, then invite players to participate for configured drops and rewards.

> Commands and features on 1MoreBlock depend on the current server configuration, world, and your permissions. A command listed here may be limited to certain ranks or contexts.

## What it adds

- Vote, schedule, or command-triggered pinata events.
- Custom pinata appearance, health, movement, announcements, and location.
- Participation, hit, kill, and drop/reward configuration.
- Placeholder and hologram integrations in supported builds.

## Commands

This feature does not require a general player command. It appears automatically, through another menu, or through staff-created content.

## Getting started

1. Watch for the event announcement and location instructions.
2. Travel to the event area using the published route.
3. Participate normally and leave space for other players.

## Player notes

- The event schedule, trigger threshold, location, damage rules, and rewards are 1MoreBlock-specific.
- Normal PvP, protection, item, and anti-cheat rules still apply.
- Do not use automation or unintended mechanics to monopolize an event.
