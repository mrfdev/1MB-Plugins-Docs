FastAsyncWorldEdit (FAWE) is the high-performance WorldEdit implementation used for authorized building and maintenance. It is not a general survival tool; access is limited because edits can affect very large areas.

> Commands and features on 1MoreBlock depend on the current server configuration, world, and your permissions. A command listed here may be limited to certain ranks or contexts.

## What it adds

- WorldEdit selections and region operations.
- Copy, paste, schematics, generation, brushes, masks, transforms, and patterns.
- Edit queues and performance controls for large operations.
- Undo/history support within configured limits.

## Commands

This feature does not require a general player command. It appears automatically, through another menu, or through staff-created content.

## Getting started

1. Request builder access through the normal staff process.
2. Practice selections and small edits in an approved build world.
3. Always inspect selection size and use undoable operations.

## Player notes

- WorldEdit commands generally begin with `//` and are not normal player commands.
- A malformed large edit can damage builds or affect performance.
- SelectionVisualizer may show selection boundaries for authorized builders.
