# CoreProtect

CoreProtect is 1MoreBlock's block-history and rollback system. It quietly records supported world changes so staff can investigate grief, accidental damage, and suspicious activity.

> Commands and features on 1MoreBlock depend on the current server configuration, world, and your permissions. A command listed here may be limited to certain ranks or contexts.

## What it adds

- Block placement and removal logging.
- Container, inventory, entity, sign, and interaction logs where supported.
- Targeted lookups by player, time, radius, action, and material.
- Previewable rollback and restore workflows.

## Commands

This feature does not require a general player command. It appears automatically, through another menu, or through staff-created content.

## Getting started

1. Note the exact location, world, and approximate time of an incident.
2. Do not rebuild a large damaged area until staff have inspected it.
3. Report involved player names and affected containers without publicly accusing anyone.

## Player notes

- CoreProtect is a staff investigation tool; players do not need its commands.
- A log entry is evidence of an action, not by itself proof of intent.
- Rollback scope must be carefully reviewed to avoid undoing unrelated work.
