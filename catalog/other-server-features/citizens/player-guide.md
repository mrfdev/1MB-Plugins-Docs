# Citizens

Citizens is the NPC platform behind staff-created characters around 1MoreBlock. Players normally interact with those NPCs by clicking them; the commands for creating and editing NPCs are staff tools.

> Commands and features on 1MoreBlock depend on the current server configuration, world, and your permissions. A command listed here may be limited to certain ranks or contexts.

## What it adds

- Persistent named NPCs with skins, poses, equipment, and traits.
- Click interactions supplied by Citizens traits or integrating plugins.
- Navigation, look-close behavior, and protected display characters.
- API used by other plugins to attach menus, shops, dialogue, or actions.

## Commands

This feature does not require a general player command. It appears automatically, through another menu, or through staff-created content.

## Getting started

1. Approach a server NPC.
2. Read its name, hologram, or nearby instructions.
3. Left- or right-click as indicated; report the NPC name and location if nothing happens.

## Player notes

- NPCs are not normal players or mobs.
- An NPC may only work in a specific world, game state, or interaction hand.
- Do not try to move, trap, or damage server NPCs.
