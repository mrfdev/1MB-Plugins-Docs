# MobFarmManager

MobFarmManager applies configured limits around chunks and farms so one dense setup cannot overwhelm the server. Players usually experience it through placement/spawn denials or cleanup messages rather than through commands.

> Commands and features on 1MoreBlock depend on the current server configuration, world, and your permissions. A command listed here may be limited to certain ranks or contexts.

## What it adds

- Per-chunk or grouped limits for configured entity types.
- Combined limits for categories such as animals or monsters.
- Optional hopper, spawner, block, and farm-component controls.
- Automated cleanup and staff diagnostics for dense chunks.

## Commands

This feature does not require a general player command. It appears automatically, through another menu, or through staff-created content.

## Getting started

1. Spread large farms across appropriate space.
2. Read any limit message before adding more entities or components.
3. Ask staff with world and coordinates if a reasonable farm is unexpectedly limited.

## Player notes

- Named, tamed, baby, equipped, or special mobs can have different handling depending on configuration.
- BentoBox Limits may separately enforce island-wide caps.
- Bypassing a denial with repeated spawning or placement can cause item loss and is not allowed.
