# InfiniCake

InfiniCake adds reusable cake behavior for configured special cakes. It is a small quality-of-life feature used where 1MoreBlock wants a cake to remain available rather than being consumed permanently.

> Commands and features on 1MoreBlock depend on the current server configuration, world, and your permissions. A command listed here may be limited to certain ranks or contexts.

## What it adds

- Infinite or replenishing cake blocks.
- Configurable item, crafting, or anvil creation behavior.
- Normal cake eating interaction for players.
- Useful support for public areas, celebrations, and decorative builds.

## Commands

This feature does not require a general player command. It appears automatically, through another menu, or through staff-created content.

## Getting started

1. Find or obtain an InfiniCake through the configured 1MoreBlock source.
2. Place it only where you are allowed to build.
3. Eat slices normally and allow its configured refill behavior to occur.

## Player notes

- Not every cake item or cake block is necessarily an InfiniCake.
- Protection plugins can prevent placing or eating cake in some areas.
- Breaking a special cake may not preserve its special state; check before moving one.
